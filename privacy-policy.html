1. Introduction

Welcome to Camply ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Camply (the "App").

By using our App, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the practices described in this policy, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Contact Information

  • Email Address: We collect your email address for account creation, authentication, password recovery, and communication purposes.

2.2 Location Data

  • Precise Location: GPS coordinates for campsite discovery, navigation, trip planning, and location-based recommendations.
  • Coarse Location: General area information for weather forecasts, regional campsite suggestions, and analytics.

2.3 User Content

  • Photos and Videos: Images you upload for campsite reviews, trip documentation, and sharing experiences.
  • Reviews and Ratings: Your campsite reviews, ratings, and feedback.
  • Trip Notes: Personal notes, itineraries, and trip planning content.
  • Custom Checklists: Personalized packing and planning lists.
  • Search History: Your campsite searches and filter preferences for improved recommendations.
  • Customer Support Data: Communications with our support team, including feedback and support requests.

2.4 Identifiers

  • User ID: Unique identifiers associated with your account for authentication and data association.
  • Device ID: Device identifiers for push notifications, analytics, and app functionality.

2.5 Usage Data

  • Product Interaction: How you interact with the App, including features used, time spent, and navigation patterns.
  • Other Usage Data: App performance data, error logs, and usage analytics.

2.6 Diagnostics

  • Crash Data: Information about app crashes and errors to improve stability.
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics including load times and responsiveness.
  • Other Diagnostic Data: Technical information about your device and app performance.
